3 DAYS 2 NIGHTS KUALA LUMPUR TOUR

Malaysia is a country that perfectly combines man-made architecture with natural beauty. It is one of the best places in the world for a summer vacation. Learn about the local culture, and of course, don't forget to snorkel and dive on one of the most beautiful islands on earth. Enjoy the most fascinating travelling experience in Kuala Lumpur, at the most affordable price.

Day 1
  • Upon your arrival at the Kuala Lumpur International Airport, you will be greeted by our representative
    who will drive you to the Sunway Putra hotel.
  • Check in at the hotel and relax, you will then be escorted to a local sightseeing tour.
  • Your first stop will be the Sri Maha Mariamman Temple, which is the oldest Hindu Temple in Kuala Lumpur.
  • Next, get immersed in Malaysia's culture by visitng the National Museum and Batu Caves.
  • At night, get a taste of local cuisine at local night markets.
Day 2
  • After a hearty breakfast, head to the Sunway Lagoon Theme Park for a full day of fun.
  • The Amusement Park, the Water Park, the Wildlife Park, the Extreme Park,
    and the Scream Park are the five zones of this amusement park.
  • Water slides, surf beach, river ride, and pirate ships are all available for your enjoyment.
  • Enjoy your night time at Petronas Twin Tower to get a glimpse of the tallest twin tower in the world.
  • Return to your hotel for a restful night's sleep.
Day 3
  • After breakfast, head out for a half-day city tour and visit the most famous tourist attractions of Kuala Lumpur.
  • You will be visiting famous KL attractions including China Town, Jalan Alor and Aquaria KLCC.
  • After a busy day, head back to the hotel to have a good night's sleep.
