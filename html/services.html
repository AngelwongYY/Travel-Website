OUR SERVICES

Leave it all to Wanderlust Travels

Pack your bags. We’re going on vacation!

Wanderlust Travel offers a wide variety of services which includes air ticket and accommodation reservations, car hire, travel insurance, transfers, visas and more. We provide a one-stop solution for all our wanderlusters. Leave the rest to us, you just need to bring along your luggage, and prepare your mind for an once in a lifetime experience.

Flight Reservations

Wanderlust Travels offers cost effective flight reservations to all destinations. Preference for any particular airline is of course taken into consideration whenever reservations are made.

Car Rental

Wanderlust Travels is an agent for a number of internationally recognized car rental companies. A mutually beneficial relationship exists with Europcar, Budget and Avis from with special rates which are passed onto our customers.
